# MOOV HR Burn Smart Coach + HR Tracking

The device pairs with the MOOV mobile app and provides:

- Real-time heart rate tracking
- Heart rate zone coaching
- Calorie burn tracking
- Interval-based workout guidance

## Sensors & Data Collected

| Sensor | Data Type | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Optical HR (PPG) | Heart Rate (BPM) | Continuous |
| Motion Sensor | Movement data | Used for workout detection |
| Accelerometer | Activity intensity | Integrated with HR zones |

## Data Export

⚠️ Limited direct raw data export support.

Available through:
- MOOV mobile app summary
- Third-party integrations (historically supported Apple Health & Google Fit)
MOOV HR Burn does not support raw data export or API-based retrieval.

## Battery & Hardware

- Rechargeable battery
- Battery life: ~6 hours active training
- Charging: USB magnetic dock
- Water resistant (sweat proof)

## How to Use

### 1. Wear Placement
- Strap device on the forearm (not wrist)
- Ensure tight skin contact

### 2. Pair with Mobile App
- Download MOOV app (historically available on iOS/Android)
- Connect via Bluetooth

### 3. Start Workout
- Select training mode (HIIT, Cardio, Fat Burn, etc.)
- Follow real-time coaching

### 4. Review Data
- Session summary in app
- View HR zones and calorie burn

---

## Research & Use Case Context

MOOV HR Burn was designed for:

- HIIT training optimization
- Fat-burning zone training
- Cardio conditioning
- Coaching-based workouts

It was not designed primarily for:

- Sleep tracking
- GPS outdoor tracking
- Long-term passive monitoring

MOOV HR Burn produces **aggregated session-level heart rate and training zone data** rather than raw physiological signals.

It is suitable for:
- Fitness behavior analysis
- Training intensity evaluation
- Coaching effectiveness research

It is not suitable for:
- Advanced physiological modeling
- HRV-based stress research
- Digital biomarker development
