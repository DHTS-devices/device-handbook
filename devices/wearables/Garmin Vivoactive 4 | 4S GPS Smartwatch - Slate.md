# 1. Overview

- Device name:
  Garmin Vivoactive 4 / Vivoactive 4S

- Full name:
  Garmin Vivoactive 4 | 4S GPS Smartwatch - Slate

- Device type:
  GPS Smartwatch / Wearable Physiological Monitoring Device

- Category:
  Wearables & Physiological Monitoring

- Manufacturer:
  [Garmin](https://www.garmin.com?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

- Overview:
  The Garmin Vivoactive 4 / 4S is a smartwatch designed for:

  - physiological monitoring
  - activity tracking
  - sleep tracking
  - exercise science
  - wellness monitoring
  - wearable sensing
  - health research
  - GPS mobility tracking

  Unlike Fitbit devices that are designed more for:

  ```text
  general consumer wellness
  ```

  Garmin devices focus more on:

  ```text
  FITNESS
  +
  PHYSIOLOGICAL METRICS
  +
  EXERCISE DATA
  ```

  The Vivoactive 4 series combines:

  - heart rate monitoring
  - Pulse Ox (SpO₂)
  - respiration tracking
  - sleep tracking
  - GPS
  - stress estimation
  - HRV-related wellness metrics
  - activity monitoring

  into one wearable platform. 

  Compared with research-grade devices:

  ```text
  Hexoskin
  or
  Empatica E4
  ```

  Garmin is:

  ```text
  LESS CLINICAL
  BUT
  VERY PRACTICAL
  ```

  for:

  ```text
  LONG-TERM DAILY MONITORING
  ```

# 5. What Signals Does It Collect?

---

## Heart Rate (HR)

Uses:

```text
Optical PPG sensor
```

Measures:

- resting HR
- exercise HR
- daily HR trends

Can also connect to:

```text
Chest ECG strap
```

such as:

```text
Polar H10
```

for more accurate HR during exercise. Garmin prioritizes chest strap data when paired. 

---

## GPS Tracking

Tracks:

```text
latitude
longitude
speed
distance
route
```

Supports:

```text
GPS
+
GLONASS
+
Galileo
```

Useful for:
- walking studies
- movement research
- mobility analysis. 

---

## Pulse Ox (SpO₂)

Measures:

```text
blood oxygen estimation
```

Useful for:
- sleep research
- recovery monitoring
- altitude adaptation

Important:

```text
NOT MEDICAL GRADE
```

---

## Respiration Tracking

Estimates:

```text
breathing rate
```

using:
```text
HR variability patterns
```

Useful for:
- wellness
- exercise recovery
- stress studies.

---

## Stress Monitoring

Garmin estimates:

```text
stress level
```

based on:

```text
HRV
+
physiology
```

Good for:

```text
trend analysis
```

but not diagnosis.

---

## Body Battery

One of Garmin’s unique features.

Workflow:

```text
sleep
+
stress
+
HRV
+
activity
↓
energy estimate
```

Helps estimate:

```text
fatigue
vs
recovery
```. 

---

## Sleep Tracking

Tracks:

- sleep duration
- sleep timing
- overnight HR
- Pulse Ox
- movement

Useful for:
- circadian rhythm research
- wellness studies
