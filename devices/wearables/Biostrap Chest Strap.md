# 8. IMPORTANT: Skin Contact Matters

This matters A LOT.

Chest strap works best when:

```text
GOOD SKIN CONTACT
```

Before wearing:

Recommended:

```text
slightly wet electrodes
```

or:

```text
electrode gel
```

especially:

```text
dry environments
```

Why?

Dry contact can cause:

```text
poor HR signal
signal drop
```

This is one of the biggest beginner mistakes.
