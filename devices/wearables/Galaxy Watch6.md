---

Uses:

```text

```

Measures:

- resting HR
- exercise HR
- daily HR trends

Useful for:
- exercise physiology
- stress monitoring
- wellness studies

---

## ECG

The Galaxy Watch6 supports:

```text

```

Useful for:

- rhythm monitoring
- arrhythmia screening
- cardiovascular tracking

Important:

```text
NOT CLINICAL ECG
```

but useful for:

```text
trend monitoring
```

instead of diagnosis.

Measures:


- sleep studies
- wellness monitoring
- recovery tracking

Important:

```text

```

not medical-grade.

```text

```

Useful for:
