# PICO 4 Enterprise

---

# 1. Overview

- Device name:
  PICO 4 Enterprise

- Device type:
  Standalone VR / Mixed Reality Headset

- Category:
  XR / VR / AR

- Manufacturer:
  PICO

- Overview:
  The PICO 4 Enterprise is a standalone XR headset designed mainly for:
  - enterprise training
  - education
  - simulation
  - VR collaboration
  - medical training
  - research
  - Unity/Unreal XR development

  Compared to the normal PICO 4 consumer version, the Enterprise model includes:
  - face tracking
  - eye tracking
  - enterprise device management
  - kiosk mode
  - business deployment features

  The headset can work:
  - standalone
  - PC VR streaming
  - wireless VR
  - tethered VR

  without requiring external base stations.

# 7. STEP-BY-STEP First Setup

---

## STEP 1 — Charge Device

Before first use:
- fully charge headset
- charge controllers

---

## STEP 2 — Power On

Press power button.

Headset starts into:
```text
PICO operating system
```

---

## STEP 3 — Connect Wi-Fi

Go to:

```text
Settings
→ Wi-Fi
```

Connect to stable network.

Required for:
- updates
- apps
- PC streaming

---

## STEP 4 — Pair Controllers

Usually automatic.

If not:

1. Open controller settings
2. Hold pairing buttons
3. Wait for connection

---

## STEP 5 — Define Play Area

Inside headset:

1. Scan room
2. Set floor height
3. Draw play boundary

This creates:

```text
Guardian Boundary
```

for safety.

# 8. Understanding Tracking

The PICO 4 Enterprise uses:

```text
Inside-out tracking
```

Meaning:

the headset tracks movement using onboard cameras.

No external sensors or base stations needed.

Best environment:
- good lighting
- visible room features
- minimal mirrors
