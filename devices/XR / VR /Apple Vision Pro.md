# Apple Vision Pro

---

# 1. Overview

- Device name:
  Apple Vision Pro

- Device type:
  Spatial Computing Headset / Mixed Reality Headset

- Category:
  XR / VR / AR

- Manufacturer:
  Apple

- Overview:
  The Apple Vision Pro is a high-end mixed reality (MR) and spatial computing headset designed for:
  - spatial computing
  - mixed reality
  - human-computer interaction research
  - medical visualization
  - immersive training
  - simulation
  - 3D interaction
  - productivity
  - spatial UX studies

  Unlike traditional VR headsets that focus mainly on gaming, the Vision Pro is designed more as:

  ```text
  A SPATIAL COMPUTER
  ```

  instead of:

  ```text
  just a VR headset
  ```

  The headset combines:
  - ultra-high-resolution displays
  - eye tracking
  - hand tracking
  - room understanding
  - passthrough mixed reality
  - spatial audio
  - LiDAR sensing

  into one integrated XR system.

  For research labs, one of the biggest advantages is:

  ```text
  EYE TRACKING
  +
  NATURAL HAND INTERACTION
  +
  SPATIAL COMPUTING
  ```

  without requiring:
  - controllers
  - base stations
  - external sensors

# 8. IMPORTANT RESEARCH SETUP (LAB USE)

For research:

DO NOT just use default setup.

Create:

```text
STANDARDIZED CONDITIONS
```

---

## Lighting Standard

Keep:

```text
consistent room lighting
```

Reason:
tracking quality changes with lighting.

Recommended:
- moderate indoor lighting
- avoid strong backlight
- avoid dark rooms

---

## Seat Position Standard

Keep participants:
```text
same sitting position
```

Reason:
gaze angle changes affect data.

---

## Distance Standard

If studying interfaces:

keep:

```text
same UI distance
```

for every participant.

Example:

```text
2 meters fixed UI distance
```

---

## Session Calibration Rule

Always:

```text
recalibrate
every participant
```

because eye tracking differs person-to-person.
