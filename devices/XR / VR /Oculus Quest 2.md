# Oculus Quest 2 (Meta Quest 2)

---

# 1. Overview

- Device name:
  Oculus Quest 2 (later renamed Meta Quest 2)

- Device type:
  Standalone VR Headset

- Category:
  XR / VR

- Manufacturer:
  Meta (formerly Oculus)

- Overview:
  The Oculus Quest 2 is a standalone virtual reality headset designed for:
  - VR gaming
  - education
  - training
  - simulation
  - fitness
  - XR development
  - research

  Unlike traditional PC VR headsets, the Quest 2 can run:
  ```text
  WITHOUT A COMPUTER
  ```

  because the headset contains:
  - processor
  - storage
  - operating system
  - tracking system

  internally.

  The Quest 2 can work in:
  - standalone mode
  - PC VR mode
  - wireless streaming
  - tethered VR

  making it one of the most flexible VR headsets.

  The headset became extremely popular because it combines:

  ```text
  LOW COST
  +
  GOOD PERFORMANCE
  +
  EASY SETUP
  ```

  into one device.

# 7. STEP-BY-STEP First Setup

---

## STEP 1 — Charge Device

Before first use:
- fully charge headset
- charge controllers if rechargeable batteries are used

Controllers normally use:
```text
AA batteries
```

---

## STEP 2 — Download Meta Quest App

Install on:
- iPhone
- Android

Used for:
- setup
- account
- pairing
- app management

---

## STEP 3 — Power On Headset

Press power button.

The headset starts setup.

---

## STEP 4 — Pair Headset

Inside phone app:

1. Select:
```text
Quest 2
```

2. Enter pairing code
3. Finish setup

---

## STEP 5 — Connect Wi-Fi

Required for:
- updates
- downloads
- multiplayer
- PC streaming

---

## STEP 6 — Define Guardian Boundary

Inside headset:

1. Set floor height
2. Draw play area
3. Confirm safe space

This creates:

```text
Guardian System
```

for safety.

# 8. Understanding Tracking

The Quest 2 uses:

```text
inside-out tracking
```

using:
```text
4 cameras
```

No external sensors required.

Best environment:
- good lighting
- visible room features

Poor environments:
- dark rooms
- mirrors
- blank walls
