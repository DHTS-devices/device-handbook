# Bose Frames Alto

---

# 1. Overview

- Device name:
  Bose Frames Alto

- Device type:
  Audio Sunglasses / Smart Glasses

- Category:
  Wearables

- Manufacturer:
  Bose

- Overview:
  Bose Frames Alto are Bluetooth audio sunglasses that combine open-ear audio playback with standard sunglass functionality. Unlike traditional headphones or earbuds, the speakers are built directly into the frame arms and direct sound toward the user’s ears without covering the ears themselves.

  The Alto model uses a classic angular sunglass design and is intended for outdoor listening, hands-free calls, navigation audio, and lightweight wearable interaction.

  The device connects through Bluetooth and functions similarly to a wireless headset while maintaining open-ear awareness of the surrounding environment.

- Primary use cases:
  - Outdoor audio listening
  - Hands-free phone calls
  - Navigation audio
  - Voice assistant interaction
  - Wearable audio experiments
  - Open-ear listening research

- Best for:
  - Outdoor environments
  - Situational awareness
  - Wearable computing experiments
  - Audio interaction research
  - XR / AR wearable concepts

# 5. Main Features

- Open-ear audio playback
- Bluetooth music streaming
- Integrated microphones for calls
- Voice assistant support
- One-button media control
- Directional speaker system
- Wearable audio interaction
- Bose AR compatibility

- Feature notes:
  Unlike earbuds or headphones, Bose Frames leave the ears uncovered. This allows the user to hear surrounding environmental sounds while still listening to music or navigation audio.

  The speakers are positioned inside the frame arms and direct sound toward the wearer’s ears. Nearby people may still hear some sound leakage in quiet environments.

  The device is more suitable for outdoor environments than quiet indoor spaces. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}

# 11. Troubleshooting

## Device Not Connecting

Possible causes:
- Bluetooth disabled
- Device already paired elsewhere
- Low battery

Possible solutions:
- Restart Bluetooth
- Re-pair device
- Recharge device
- Restart phone

---

## Low Audio Volume

Possible causes:
- Outdoor environmental noise
- Low device volume
- Speaker obstruction

Possible solutions:
- Increase playback volume
- Reduce surrounding noise
- Clean speaker openings

---

## Sound Leakage

Possible causes:
- High playback volume
- Quiet indoor environment

Possible solutions:
- Reduce volume
- Use outdoors or in noisier environments

---

## Charging Issues

Possible causes:
- Dirty charging contacts
- Damaged charging cable
- Battery degradation

Possible solutions:
- Clean contacts carefully
- Replace cable
- Verify charger power source

# 12. Notes

- Bose Frames Alto are wearable audio devices rather than full AR smart glasses.
- The device does not include a display or camera.
- The main focus of the product is open-ear audio interaction.
- Compared to headphones, the device provides better environmental awareness but less audio isolation.
- Sound quality is generally stronger for podcasts, calls, and spoken audio than for bass-heavy music. :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}

- DHTS notes:
