# 1. Overview

- Device name:
  RØDE AI-Micro

- Device type:
  Dual-Channel USB Audio Interface

- Category:
  Video & Audio

- Manufacturer:
  RØDE

- Overview:
  The RØDE AI-Micro is a compact dual-channel USB audio interface designed for connecting microphones, headsets, and audio devices directly to computers, tablets, or smartphones.

  Unlike a standard USB sound adapter, the AI-Micro is designed specifically for high-quality portable recording workflows and mobile content creation. The device supports two microphone inputs simultaneously and provides low-latency digital audio conversion for recording and streaming applications.

  The AI-Micro is commonly used for:
  - podcast recording
  - mobile interviews
  - streaming
  - field recording
  - online meetings
  - mobile journalism
  - educational recording
  - AI speech recording workflows

  The interface is especially useful for creators or researchers who need portable audio recording with better sound quality than a built-in laptop or phone microphone input.

# 4. Hardware Information

- Audio inputs:
  2 × 3.5mm auto-sensing microphone inputs

- Audio outputs:
  USB digital audio output

- Connectivity:
  USB-C

- Supported devices:
  - Windows
  - macOS
  - iPhone
  - iPad
  - Android devices

- Supported microphones:
  - Lavalier microphones
  - Wireless microphone receivers
  - Headsets
  - TRS microphones
  - TRRS microphones

- Input detection:
  Automatic TRS/TRRS sensing

- Power source:
  USB powered

- Weight:
  Approximately 13 g

- Recording capability:
  Dual-channel simultaneous recording

- Latency:
  Low-latency audio interface

- Hardware notes:
  The device is extremely compact and designed for portable workflows rather than large studio setups.

  Unlike larger desktop audio interfaces, the AI-Micro does not include:
  - XLR inputs
  - onboard gain knobs
  - phantom power
  - large analog controls

  Instead, the device focuses on lightweight mobile recording and USB audio conversion. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
