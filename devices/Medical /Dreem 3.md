# Dreem 3

---

# 1. Overview

- Device name:
  Dreem 3

- Device type:
  Sleep EEG Headband / Sleep Monitoring Device / Neurophysiology Wearable

- Category:
  Neurophysiology & Sleep Monitoring

- Manufacturer:
  Dreem (later acquired by Beacon Biosignals)

- Overview:
  The Dreem 3 is a wearable EEG sleep headband designed for:

  - sleep research
  - sleep staging
  - home sleep monitoring
  - sleep disorder studies
  - longitudinal sleep tracking
  - cognitive recovery studies
  - EEG sleep analysis
  - circadian rhythm research
  - remote sleep studies

  Unlike consumer sleep devices such as:

  ```text
  Fitbit
  Apple Watch
  Oura Ring
  ```

  the Dreem 3 focuses on:

  ```text
  EEG-BASED SLEEP MEASUREMENT
  ```

  instead of only:

  ```text
  movement
  +
  heart rate estimation
  ```

  The Dreem 3 is often described as:

  ```text
  A PORTABLE SLEEP LAB
  ```

  because it can collect:

  ```text
  EEG sleep data
  ```

  at home without requiring a full:

  ```text
  polysomnography (PSG)
  ```

  sleep lab setup. Multiple studies validated the Dreem 3 against PSG and found strong performance for sleep staging and longitudinal sleep monitoring. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

  Think of it as:

  ```text
  EEG HEADBAND
  ↓
  HOME SLEEP STUDY
  ↓
  AUTOMATED SLEEP STAGING
  ```

# 8. IMPORTANT: Proper Placement Matters

This matters A LOT.

Headband should sit:

```text
FOREHEAD
TO BACK OF HEAD
```

so electrodes contact:

```text
frontal
+
occipital regions
```

Poor fit causes:

```text
bad EEG
poor sleep staging
signal dropout
```

Before sleep:

Always check:

```text
good contact
```.
