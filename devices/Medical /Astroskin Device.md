# 8. IMPORTANT: Garment Fit Matters

This matters A LOT.

The garment should fit:

```text
SNUG
```

but:

```text
NOT TOO TIGHT
```

Why?

Because:

```text
ECG
+
respiration sensors
```

require:

```text
GOOD BODY CONTACT
```

Loose fit causes:

```text
poor ECG
bad respiration signal
movement artifact
```

This is one of the most common problems.
